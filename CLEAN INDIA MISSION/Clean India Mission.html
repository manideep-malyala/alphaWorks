CLEAN INDIA MISSION



Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan Is A National Campaign By The Government Of India, Covering 4,041 Statutory Cities And Towns, To Clean The Streets, Roads And Infrastructure Of The Country.

The Campaign Was Officially Launched On 2, OCt, 2014 At Rajghat, New Delhi, By Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

It Is India's Biggest Ever Cleanliness Drive And 3 Million Govt Employees And School And College Students Of India Participated In This Event.
